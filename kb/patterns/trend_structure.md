# Trend structure (V1 sample)

A simple bullish stack: price above a faster moving average and the faster average above a slower one. The opposite stack suggests bearish bias.

Use session range versus ATR: a tight session range relative to ATR can precede expansion; do not confuse quiet tape with a guaranteed directional move.

Support and resistance from recent swing pivots are provisional until tested with volume and time at the level.

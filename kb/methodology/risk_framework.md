# Risk framework (V1 sample)

When volatility_regime is **high**, reduce position sizing and widen invalidation buffers. Prefer smaller clips over full size.

When trend_regime is **neutral** and ATR% is elevated, treat breakouts as suspect until a 5m close confirms beyond the level.

Always assume gaps and event risk; never state guaranteed outcomes.
